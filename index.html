Privacy Policy for BuildExact Last updated: 2026 This privacy policy applies to the mobile application BuildExact, developed and published by Rudy Sijbers. BuildExact is a construction calculator app designed for builders, contractors, and DIY users. It allows users to calculate area (m²), volume (m³), material estimates, and organize projects. 1. Data Collection BuildExact does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app does not require account creation and does not request personal information such as name, email address, or location. 2. Local Data Storage All data created within the app (such as projects and calculations) is stored locally on the user’s device. This data is not transmitted to external servers and remains fully under the user’s control. 3. Third-Party Services BuildExact uses Google Play services for app distribution and in-app purchases. These services may collect certain data as described in their own privacy policies: https://policies.google.com/privacy BuildExact does not directly access or control this data. 4. In-App Purchases The app offers optional in-app purchases to unlock PRO features. All payments are securely handled by Google Play. BuildExact does not process or store payment information. 5. Children’s Privacy BuildExact is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13, but it does not knowingly collect any personal data from users of any age. 6. Data Security Since BuildExact does not collect or transmit personal data, there is no risk of personal data exposure through the app itself. 7. Changes to This Policy This privacy policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. 8.
