Privacy Policy for BuildExact This privacy policy applies to the mobile application BuildExact, developed by Rudy Sijbers. BuildExact is a construction calculator app that allows users to perform calculations such as area, volume, paint estimation, and project organization. Data Collection BuildExact does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All calculations and project data are stored locally on the user's device and are not transmitted to any external servers. Third-Party Services BuildExact uses Google Play services for app distribution and in-app purchases. These services may collect data as described in their own privacy policies. User Data The app does not require account creation and does not collect personal information such as name, email address, or location.
